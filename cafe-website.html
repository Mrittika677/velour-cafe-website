Our Story

Crafted from
Passion,
Poured with Soul

Velour began in a small sunlit corner of the city — a single espresso machine, a dream, and an unwavering belief that coffee could be more than a morning ritual. It could be a moment. A memory. An experience.

Every bean we source tells a story of its origin. From the misty highlands of Ethiopia to the volcanic slopes of Guatemala, we travel the world to bring you cups that carry the soul of their land.

Craft Moments
With Us

At Velour, every role is a craft. We're a team of people who believe deeply in the art of hospitality — in the quiet pleasure of a perfect cup, the warmth of a familiar face, and the beauty of a space made for slow mornings.

Why Velour

More Than a Job.
A Calling.

☕

Craft First

We train every team member in the full art of specialty coffee — from bean origin to extraction science. Whatever your role, you'll understand the craft deeply.

🌱

Grow With Us

We believe in promoting from within. Many of our managers started as baristas. Growth paths are real, encouraged, and built into your journey with us.

🤝

True Community

The team at Velour feels like family. We celebrate together, support one another, and create an atmosphere where every person feels seen and valued.

✦

Premium Perks

Competitive pay, daily staff coffee, team meals, quarterly retreats, professional development budget, and flexible scheduling that respects your life outside work.

🎨

Creative Culture

We encourage ideas from every corner of the team. Seasonal menus, new drinks, event concepts — your voice matters and your creativity is actively invited.

💛

Hospitality Values

Warmth is our foundation. We hire for heart first, skill second — because the right attitude creates the moments that guests remember for years.
